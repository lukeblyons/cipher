Senior Security Engineer (Platform)

Full‑time Security & Platform

Design zero‑knowledge patterns, harden networks, and build key ceremonies to protect high‑value systems.

Location: Remote — US preferred (Denver time overlap 4+ hours)
Compensation: Base $170,000–$210,000 + benefits + equipment & learning stipends

Responsibilities

  • Architect and implement zero‑knowledge data flows (CSFLE/Queryable Encryption).
  • Harden identity, network, and secrets: FIDO2, mTLS, PrivateLink/WireGuard, Vault/KMS/HSM.
  • Define and run key ceremonies (CloudHSM/YubiHSM/YubiKey); rotation and escrow policies.
  • Build monitoring, detections, and IR runbooks; participate in on‑call (reasonable rotations).
  • Lead security reviews, tabletop drills, and post‑incident reports.

Requirements

  • 5+ years in security/platform/SRE; strong Linux, networking, and IaC.
  • Hands‑on with TLS/mTLS, RBAC/ABAC, SIEM/telemetry, backup/restore testing.
  • Experience with MongoDB/Postgres hardening; familiarity with CSFLE or blind indexes.
  • Excellent written communication; calm, methodical delivery.

Nice to have

  • Nitro Enclaves/Confidential VMs; WORM/immutable backups; PrivateLink patterns.
  • Compliance readiness (SOC 2/ISO 27001) without cargo‑culting.
