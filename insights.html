Case Brief

Principal Data Vault in a Privacy‑Favorable Jurisdiction

Consolidate highly sensitive records and communications under client‑held keys with jurisdictional control and immutable backups.

UHNW Principal 3–4 min read Updated 2025‑08‑29

Objective

Build a private, searchable repository for documents, communications, and media with zero plaintext exposure to vendors.

Constraints

No public endpoints; keys must remain with client (HSM + YubiKey). Residency restricted to a single European privacy‑favorable jurisdiction. Rapid restore required.

Approach

CSFLE/Queryable Encryption for PII; private NAS (ZFS + snapshots) paired with MongoDB cluster; PrivateLink‑only admin; blind indexes for exact‑match and prefix queries; SIEM with redacted logs.

Security Posture

Client‑owned HSM; dual‑control key ceremony; mTLS; least‑privilege RBAC; immutable/WORM backups; quarterly restore tests.

Outcome

Single source of truth; RTO/RPO met (1h/15m); reduced vendor sprawl; cryptographic erasure capability for specific datasets.

Delivered

Architecture pack, key ceremony guide, runbooks (backup/restore, IR), monitoring dashboards, UAT records.

Family Office — Multi‑Entity Reporting & Permissions

Unify reporting across entities with strict role‑based access, entity‑scoped schemas, and jurisdiction‑locked replicas.

Family Office 3–4 min read Updated 2025‑08‑29

Objective

Consolidate financial/operational data across multiple entities with entity‑level permissions and auditability.

Constraints

Residency bound to two jurisdictions; segregation between entities; zero‑knowledge for sensitive fields.

Approach

Postgres for reporting views + MongoDB operational store; CSFLE + deterministic encryption for joins; scheduled report packs; PrivateLink between services.

Security Posture

Per‑entity RBAC; time‑bound break‑glass; session recording for admin; immutable backups in each jurisdiction.

Outcome

Faster monthly closes; clean audit trail; measurable SLOs; reduced spreadsheet risk.

Delivered

Data model by entity, ETL pipelines, access attestations, reporting templates, SLO dashboard.
